Project Name: CivicConnect AI
Tagline: Empowering Citizens, Simplifying Governance

Core Features (Current Implementation):
1. LawBot – AI Legal Aid: Explains laws in plain language and generates basic legal documents like complaint letters or agreements.
2. Talk2Gov – Scheme Finder: Identifies eligible government schemes based on user profile and auto-fills application forms.
3. Local Language Accessibility Assistant: Translates and simplifies complex documents or government portals into regional languages.
4. Unified Chat Interface: Single conversational interface for all queries with voice input/output support for accessibility.

Future Feature (Planned Update):
Community Resource Finder: Will locate local services like food banks, shelters, clinics, and community events with maps and calendars.
